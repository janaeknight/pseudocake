Boring Vanilla Cake



     Name:                   Boring Vanilla Cake

     Batter Ingredients:     420g cake flour
                             1 teaspoon salt
                             1 teaspoon baking powder
                             3/4 teaspoon baking soda
                             345g unsalted butter
                             400g granulated sugar
                             3 large eggs
                             2 egg whites
                             1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
                             360ml buttermilk

     Frosting Ingredients:   345g unsalted butter
                             720g confectioners' sugar
                             80ml whole milk or heavy cream
                             1 and 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
                             1/8 teaspoon salt
