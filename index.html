Boring Vanilla Cake



     Name:                   Boring Vanilla Cake

     Batter Ingredients:     420g cake flour
                             1 teaspoon salt
                             1 teaspoon baking powder
                             3/4 teaspoon baking soda
                             345g unsalted butter
                             400g granulated sugar
                             3 large eggs
                             2 egg whites
                             1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
                             360ml buttermilk

     Frosting Ingredients:   345g unsalted butter
                             720g confectioners' sugar
                             80ml whole milk or heavy cream
                             1 and 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
                             1/8 teaspoon salt

Cooking Instructions


Step 1:    Preheat oven to 177*C.
Step 2:    Mix batter ingredients together.
Step 4:    Pour batter evenly into cake pans.
Step 5:    Let cake bake for 23-26 minutes or until baked.
Step 6:    Take cake out and let cool.
Step 7:    Carefully whip frosting ingredients into a whip. ;)
Step 8:    After cake is cool, put frosting on it.
Step 9:    Eat Cake.
